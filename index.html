01: WWF Astroni - Immersive Journey

For the last challenge of the Apple Developer Academy, we aimed to create this app to enhance the experience of the beautiful site of Astroni to provide visitors with navigation with points of interest, some AR experiences, 2d & 3d model to locate themselves and find the directions to their track or points of interest. I created the assets for this project including the accurate topographical interactive 3d map of Astroni, AR experiences, UI/UX, flow and app icon.
[Link to Github]






Testing of 3d map and AR of lava within the app




02: Buggiess


Buggiess is a gamified app created after an intense research and testing phase about Entomophobia (fear of insects), it's aimed for children to gradually familiarise them with insects' anatomies with the intention of gradual exposure therapy to help them avoid developing Entomophobia as a grown up. Designed all the assets including the characters, the UI/UX, flow, animated and interactive 3d models, AR experience of all the insects, promotional posters, app icon and screens for App Store.

04: Planet Buddy!
